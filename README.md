## About the Mod:
This adapter enables the T-GT Wheel to be used as a standalone USB device on PC. 
<br>
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/t-gt-wheel-usb.jpg" width="350">

### Advanced Mode Enabled:
All of the wheel’s functionalities are preserved: buttons, rotary encoders, joysticks, and paddle shifters.

### IMPORTANT - Wheel compatibility:
The GT Wheel is not compatible with this adapter, as it is equipped with the latest quick release system. The T-GT Wheel included with the T-GT and T-GT II bundles is the only wheel compatible with this mod.
<br>
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/not-compatible.jpg" width="350"> 

### Installation guide:
###### 1. Unscrew the four screws securing the center cap.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/1.jpg" width="400"> 

###### 2. Unscrew the M3 screws.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/2.jpg" width="400">

###### 3. Unscrew the M4 screws.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/3.jpg" width="400"> 

###### 4. Remove the wheel rim.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/4.jpg" width="400"> 

###### 5. Unscrew the four screws securing the front panel.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/5.jpg" width="400"> 

###### 6. Open the hub.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/6.jpg" width="400">

###### 7. Disconnect the cable. The wire may be cut or unsoldered.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/7.jpg" width="400">

###### 8. Unscrew the wheel connector from the quick release.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/8.jpg" width="400">

###### 9. Disassemble the quick release.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/9.jpg" width="400">

###### 10. Reconnect the cable. Insulate the exposed wire or resolder it.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/10.jpg" width="400">

###### 11. Attach your 6×70 mm quick release (not shown) to the USB Adapter Mod, then mount the assembly onto the wheel.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/11.jpg" width="400"> 

###### 12. Connect the wheel's motherboard to the adapter's port then screw it.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/12.jpg" width="400"> 

###### 13. Reassemble the wheel.
<img src="T-GT Manual Images/13.jpg" width="400">
